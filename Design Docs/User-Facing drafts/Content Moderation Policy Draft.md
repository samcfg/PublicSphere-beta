# **Content Moderation Policy Draft**

## **What We Don't Allow**

**Comments and posts will be removed for:**

* Personal attacks, harassment, or threats toward other users  
* Spam, excessive self-promotion, or commercial solicitation  
* Content unrelated to the source being discussed  
* Deliberately false or misleading information

**Threads may be locked when:**

* Discussions become consistently hostile  
* Conversations repeatedly go off-topic

**User accounts may be suspended for:**

* Repeated violations after warnings  
* Creating multiple accounts to evade moderation  
* Harassment across multiple discussions  
* Posting illegal content

## **What Happens If You're Suspended**

* You can still log in to access your account settings  
* You cannot view or participate in any article discussions  
* You can contact us to appeal the suspension

## **Source Content Standards**

Sources may be removed if they contain:

* Copyrighted material without permission  
* Spam or completely inappropriate content  
* No substantive connection to claimed research or information

## **Appeals**

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error:

* Contact us at \[publicspherestaff@gmail.com\]  
* Explain why you think the action was incorrect  
* Include any relevant context we should consider  
* Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator when possible
